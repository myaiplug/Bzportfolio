Creative Systems Engineering

My journey began in the high-pressure environment of the recording studio, where I became obsessed with the physics of sound and the intricate dance between technology and human emotion.

As I pushed the limits of traditional tools, I realized that the future of media wasn't just in the art we make, but in the systems we build. This led me to transition from audio engineering to media systems design, where I now build custom AI-driven tools, automated production pipelines, and high-performance digital products.

Today, I bridge the gap between creative intuition and engineering precision. Whether I'm developing a new VST plugin, architecting a cloud-based media system, or designing a premium user experience, my goal is always the same: to create tools that empower creators and scale their vision.
